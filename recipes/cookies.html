Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These chewy, golden-brown cookies are filled with rich chocolate chips — a warm, classic dessert perfect with a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars. Mix in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Combine dry ingredients and stir into the wet mixture.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips and scoop onto a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes. Let cool slightly before serving.
