Brownie Sundae

Description

A warm brownie topped with vanilla ice cream, chocolate syrup, and a cherry on top â€” this dessert is a mouthwatering combo of hot and cold textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 batch of brownies (homemade or store-bought)
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Cherries (for topping)

Steps

  1. Warm the brownie in the microwave or oven.
  2. Place one or two scoops of ice cream on top.
  3. Drizzle generously with chocolate syrup.
  4. Add a swirl of whipped cream and top with a cherry.
  5. Serve immediately while the brownie is warm!
