Strawberry Cheesecake

Description

This creamy, rich cheesecake is topped with a layer of fresh strawberries and strawberry glaze. A dessert that’s sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (8 oz) cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1/2 cup strawberry glaze

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and butter; press into a springform pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Pour over crust and bake for 45–50 minutes. Cool completely.
  5. Top with strawberries and glaze. Chill before serving.
